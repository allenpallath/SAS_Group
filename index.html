About Us

SAS Electricals was established in the year 1997. The company is headed by Mr. V.S Sajeevan having a track record of 28 years in the electrical industry. SAS Group was instrumental in materializing many projects for Kerala Water Authority, Kerala Public Works Department and Electrical Inspectorate. Mr. V.S Sajeevan is a registered contractor in KWA, KPWD and Department Of Electrical Inspectorate Kerala. Today the company provides a broad spectrum of value added services in electrical and construction segment with top of the line professionals and state of the art equipments.
